What is stress management?

Stress management consists of a wide spectrum of techniques and psychotherapies aimed at controlling a person's level of stress, especially chronic stress, usually for the purpose of improving everyday functioning.


What are mentals signs of stress?

Mental signs of stress include increased anger, nervousness, depression, etc


What are physical signs of stress?

Physical signs of stress include fatigue, difficulty eating, headaches, etc.


What is Soduku?

Sudoku is a logic-based, combinatorial number-placement puzzle. In classic Sudoku, the objective is to fill a 9 x 9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3 x 3 subgrids that compose the grid contains all of the digits from 1 to 9


How does it help with mental stress management?

Sudoku is a game of logic, problem solving and spotting patterns. It is a brain game that helps stimulate people's cognitive abilities and gives the satisfaction of accomplishing a difficult puzzle.


How does it help with physical stress management?

The problem solving abilities and clarity that Sudoku provides may help players deal with obstacles in their daily lives more effectively, and reduce sress.
